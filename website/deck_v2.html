Who has authority over your vault

Nobody can move your funds
outside the rules you configured.

What nobody — including us — can do

  • Move funds out of your vault unilaterally
  • Awaken you while you're still pinging
  • Change who your beneficiaries are
  • Prevent beneficiaries from receiving funds
  • Push a malicious upgrade before a 7-day timelock

What you (and only you) can do

  • Ping, deposit, withdraw, reconfigure, pause
  • Designate up to 3 recovery wallets as emergency trustees
  • Cancel an awakening during the grace period
  • Transfer Ghost ownership to a new wallet
  • Decide — specifically — who inherits what, and when

The vault PDA's private key does not exist. The only way to move funds is through program logic you configured.
