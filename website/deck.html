05 — The Protocol Four actions. That's the whole thing.
How it works

A trustless, on-chain
dead-man's switch for
any SOL or SPL token.

01 — REGISTER
Ghost PDA + Vault PDA
Create your Ghost on-chain. A unique vault PDA is derived; only program logic can move its funds — no private key exists.
02 — FUND
Deposit & configure
Deposit SOL and any SPL tokens. Set up to 10 beneficiaries: who gets what, or who gets everything left.
03 — STAY ALIVE
Ping before you expire
Send a heartbeat before your interval runs out. Costs less than a cent. Miss it, and the grace period begins automatically.
04 — EXECUTE
Anyone can trigger
After grace expires, anyone can call execute_legacy. The vault PDA signs CPIs to each beneficiary. No operator, no gatekeeper.
Digital legacy first — but also travel, hospitalization, seed-phrase loss, or a contingency slice for family. Most Ghosts are pinged, cancelled, or executed long before anyone actually dies.
06 — Why Solana Not ethical decoration. Real reason.
This product does not work on another chain

Solana is the one chain
where this is actually shippable.

PDA-owned vaults

Keys that don't exist.

Vaults are owned by program-derived addresses. Their private keys cannot be generated by anyone — not the user, not the operator, not an attacker. Only program logic can move funds.

Permissionless execution

Anyone is the executor.

check_silence and execute_legacy can be called by any Solana address. If our bot disappears, beneficiaries (or any bounty hunter) can still trigger the afterlife. There is no gatekeeper.

Sub-cent heartbeats

Cheap enough for everyone.

A weekly ping costs less than $0.01. On a chain with $5 gas, this becomes a product for whales only. On Solana, anyone with a wallet can afford to stay alive.

07 — Trust Model The slide that closes the objection.
Who has authority over your vault

Nobody can move your funds
outside the rules you configured.

What nobody — including us — can do

  • Move funds out of your vault unilaterally
  • Awaken you while you're still pinging
  • Change who your beneficiaries are
  • Prevent beneficiaries from receiving funds
  • Push a malicious upgrade before a 7-day timelock

What you (and only you) can do

  • Ping, deposit, withdraw, reconfigure, pause
  • Designate up to 3 recovery wallets as emergency trustees
  • Cancel an awakening during the grace period
  • Transfer Ghost ownership to a new wallet
  • Decide — specifically — who inherits what, and when

The vault PDA's private key does not exist. The only way to move funds is through program logic you configured.
